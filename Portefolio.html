About Me

I was born in Porto on a sunny day in September, it was 1997. Michael Jackson Season, Spice Girl, Mariah Carey, and the great Leonardo DiCaprio! I grew up with the races of the village, behind the animals and the conquest of new friends, with Strawberries with Sugar with Bravo, with Roxette, with Justin Bieber, with the Linkin Park, with the boom of Hip-hop, with all that was considered astonishing for the time. Missing! When I realized I had to choose something to follow, the technology seemed to me an interesting thing. Over there I went to Technologies and Information Systems for the Web, at the Superior School of Media Arts and Design, full of dreams and ambitions. Currently attending the first semester of the third year.



Event organizer, extracurricular activities and parties by the secondary students' association, certificate collector, diplomas and medals since childhood in the swimming and other modality, delegate of class during my fantastic twelfth year, scouts throughout five years by explorer and Pioneer and thus acquiring responsibility and the importance of working as a team. Portista occasion, inventor of kitchen to elaborate dishes and satisfy the greed of others, owner of a considerable collection of books, varnishes and shoes, daily sportsman (volleyball and swimming are a vice), person who does not say not a party, a way out, chocolate, shopping or new experiences and always ready to jump in an airplane because there is nothing better in life than to know new cultures and acquire other knowledge. Nature fan who never misses a good day whether sun or rain enjoy the same accompanied by a cup of tea and a good book. Lover of a good laugh, of good friends and moments spent as a family.
